Aperol Spritz Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 parts Prosecco
  • 2 parts Aperol
  • 1 part Soda water
  • Ice
  • Orange slice for garnish

Instructions

  1. Fill a wine glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour the Prosecco, Aperol, and soda water over the ice.
  3. Stir gently to mix the ingredients.
  4. Garnish with an orange slice.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your Aperol Spritz!
