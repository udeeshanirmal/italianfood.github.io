Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (225g) spaghetti pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes

Preparation Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the spaghetti and cook according to package directions until al dente.
  3. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  4. Add minced garlic and diced onion. Cook until fragrant and onions are translucent.
  5. Add diced tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Once the spaghetti is cooked, drain and add it to the skillet with the sauce.
  7. Toss the spaghetti in the sauce until well coated.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves if desired.
