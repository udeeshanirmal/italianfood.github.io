Limoncello Recipe

Ingredients

  • 10 Lemons
  • 1 liter of vodka
  • 3 1/2 cups of water
  • 2 1/2 cups of sugar

Instructions

  1. Peel the lemons using a potato peeler, ensuring no white pith remains.
  2. Place the lemon peels in a large glass jar and add the vodka.
  3. Seal the jar and let it sit in a cool, dark place for at least a week, shaking it occasionally.
  4. After a week, prepare the syrup by boiling water and sugar until the sugar dissolves, then let it cool.
  5. Strain the vodka from the lemon peels and mix it with the cooled syrup.
  6. Store the Limoncello in the freezer and serve chilled. Enjoy!
