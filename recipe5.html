Aperol Spritz Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Aperol
  • 3 oz Prosecco
  • Splash of soda water
  • Orange slice (for garnish)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add 2 ounces of Aperol to the glass.
  3. Pour 3 ounces of Prosecco into the glass.
  4. Add a splash of soda water.
  5. Stir gently to combine the ingredients.
  6. Garnish with an orange slice.
  7. Serve and enjoy your refreshing Aperol Spritz!
