Bruschetta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4-5 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 6-8 fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 baguette or Italian bread, sliced
  • Olive oil for brushing

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, chopped onion, minced garlic, chopped basil leaves, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper. Mix gently and let it sit for about 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  2. Preheat your grill or oven to medium-high heat. Brush the bread slices with olive oil on both sides.
  3. Grill or toast the bread slices until they're golden brown and crispy on the edges.
  4. Once the bread is toasted, spoon the tomato mixture generously onto each slice.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Bruschetta!
